BMI means BODY MASS INDEX. It is the measurement of a person's tissue mass, based on their height and weight. Specifically the value of BMI, is used to categorize whether a person is underweight, normal or overweight. It is a basic method of testing health condition of a person and should not be considered as the final test for your health but is an approximate measure of your health.

**If a person is having BMI 17.8 but he does regular exrecise and physical activity then it will be considered under healthy category.
